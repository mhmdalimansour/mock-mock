Context

  • This work covers the engineering implementation of two web modules: Mother Categories Management and Priority Management. The goal is to enable full CRUD operations, category linking, and priority control for mother categories and their related categories.

  • It also covers the mobile integration where we will be updating the Get Aisles Endpoint to fetch the Mother Categories
